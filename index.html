Who we are

Most brands hide the math. We don't.

Transparent is an apparel brand built on one idea: you deserve to know exactly what you're paying for. No inflated margins disguised as "premium pricing." No vague sustainability claims. Just well-made clothes and an honest number.

Where this came from

I started Transparent because I was fed up as a consumer. The brands I depended on for everyday goods marked up their products 5 to 10 times their cost and kept the math private. Consumer values went out the window whenever there was money on the table.

Transparent is my attempt to prove you can balance profit with consumer values and still build a sustainable business. The model is radical price transparency — no more wondering "is this worth it?" When you buy from us, you see exactly where your money goes: materials, labor, operations, and yes, our margin too. No mystery markup. No information withheld.

You get the full picture, and everything you need to make a conscious choice.
