Why we show the math

The industry hides it

Most clothing brands mark up 5–10× from cost to retail. They don't disclose it because they don't have to. We think that's the wrong default.

You can decide what's fair

When you see a $52 tee, you shouldn't have to guess. With the full breakdown, you can judge for yourself whether the margin is reasonable.

It makes us accountable

Posting our cost structure publicly means we can't quietly raise margins or cut corners on materials. Transparency is a commitment, not just a feature.

Recycled, not just organic

Our cotton comes from post-industrial textile waste — GRS certified. Less water, less virgin fiber, and a supply chain story we're proud to put on the hang tag.
